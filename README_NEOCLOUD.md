# Neo Cloud Startup Simulator

An interactive educational game that teaches startup fundamentals and VC thinking through the lens of running a neo cloud provider (AI infrastructure company).

## 🎯 Purpose

This simulator helps employees and entrepreneurs understand:
- How VCs evaluate startups and make investment decisions
- Key SaaS/infrastructure metrics (CAC, LTV, gross margin, burn rate, runway)
- The dynamics of the neo cloud market (AI infrastructure providers)
- Strategic tradeoffs between growth and profitability
- Unit economics and their impact on fundraising

## 🚀 How to Play

1. **Open the game**: Simply open `neo-cloud-simulator.html` in any modern web browser
2. **Make quarterly decisions**: Each quarter, choose your strategy across 4 areas:
   - **Pricing Strategy**: Premium, market rate, or aggressive discounting
   - **Infrastructure Investment**: Performance optimization, new features, or minimal spend
   - **Sales & Marketing**: Aggressive growth, balanced approach, or lean sales
   - **Customer Focus**: Enterprise, mid-market, or SMB/startups
3. **Watch your metrics**: Track MRR, gross margin, runway, CAC/LTV ratio, and more
4. **Learn from results**: Each decision impacts your business - see the consequences play out
5. **Reach Series A**: Goal is to build a fundable business ($5M+ ARR, 50%+ margins)

## 📊 What You'll Learn

### Key VC Concepts
- **Unit Economics**: Understanding CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) vs LTV (Lifetime Value)
- **Gross Margin**: Why neo cloud providers need 50%+ margins to be attractive
- **Runway Management**: How to manage cash and when to start fundraising
- **Rule of 40**: Balancing growth rate and profitability
- **Series A Metrics**: What traction VCs expect ($1-3M ARR, 100%+ growth)

### Neo Cloud Market Dynamics
- Competitive landscape with 12+ funded providers
- Infrastructure costs vs revenue (COGS management)
- GPU availability and performance as competitive advantages
- Enterprise vs SMB customer economics
- Building defensible moats in a crowded market

## 🎮 Game Features

- **Real-time metrics dashboard** with color-coded indicators
- **Interactive tooltips** explaining each metric
- **Quarterly decision making** with clear cost/benefit tradeoffs
- **Random market events** (competitor funding, customer churn, big deals)
- **Decision impact summaries** showing how your choices affected the business
- **Educational lessons** each quarter about VC thinking
- **Multiple win/lose conditions** based on runway and performance

## 📈 Success Metrics

The game tracks realistic SaaS/infrastructure metrics:
- **MRR/ARR**: Monthly/Annual Recurring Revenue
- **Gross Margin**: Revenue minus infrastructure costs
- **Burn Rate**: Monthly cash consumption
- **Runway**: Months of cash remaining
- **MoM Growth**: Month-over-month revenue growth
- **CAC/LTV Ratio**: Customer acquisition efficiency
- **Churn Rate**: Customer retention
- **Team Size**: Headcount growth
